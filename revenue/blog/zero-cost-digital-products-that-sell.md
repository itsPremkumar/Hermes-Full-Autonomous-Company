# 9 Zero-Cost Digital Products You Can Ship This Weekend (and Actually Sell)

*Category: AI Business / Monetization · Part of the "Prem Autonomous Co" content funnel.*

Everyone says "sell a digital product." Few say which one, why it sells, or how to
build it with $0 and a laptop that's running a dozen other things. Here are nine
product types we've packaged and listed — each one produced entirely by agents, with
no ad spend, no inventory, no upfront cost.

## Why these nine
A digital product only works if it (1) solves a specific job, (2) is fast to consume,
and (3) is cheap to deliver. Every item below clears all three. They also map directly
to the templates our own agents generate, so the build cost is effectively zero.

## The list

### 1. Short-form video script packs
Creators burn out writing hooks. 50 done-for-you Reels/Shorts/TikTok scripts across 5
niches = instant value. Deliver as Markdown + CSV. Price: $9–15.

### 2. AI agent operations playbook
Founders want the "how we run a company with agents" doc. A 20-page PDF with
AGENTS.md templates, heartbeat configs, and a launch runbook sells itself to the
AI-curious. Price: $25–39.

### 3. Remotion / code-video templates
Developers who want faceless video without After Effects. JSON + config templates they
can fork. Price: $15–29.

### 4. Monetization kits
A bundle of 8 strategy docs — pricing sheets, outreach sequences, lead magnets. The
"starter stack" buyers love. Price: $29.

### 5. Cold outreach & lead-gen packs
Done-for-you email/LinkedIn sequences that get replies. Sales people pay for this
quarterly. Price: $12–19.

### 6. Job-board / gig guide
How to find AI-native remote gigs + a template board setup. Niche but evergreen.
Price: $9.

### 7. Pricing page templates
Copy-paste pricing tiers, YAML configs, and psychology notes. Tiny to make, high
perceived value. Price: $9–15.

### 8. Launch plan / teardown
A 30-day, zero-cost launch timeline with the exact posts and channels. Price: $19.

### 9. The "all-products bundle"
Package 1–8 into one zip. Price the bundle below the sum so it's an obvious yes.
Price: $49.

## The build loop (zero cost)
1. Agent drafts the asset as Markdown.
2. `package.py` zips it with a README + license.
3. Catalog entry added to `product-catalog.json`.
4. Human approves the Gumroad listing (the one gate we keep human).
5. Marketing post goes out via the content funnel.

## The honest part
None of these sell on autopilot. They sell because the content funnel (SEO posts like
this one + community posts) points qualified readers at a $0-cost product that does a
job. Automate the *production*; stay human on the *money*.

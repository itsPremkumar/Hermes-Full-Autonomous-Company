# AI Agent Service Catalog + Pricing Templates
## Customizable Tiered Pricing for AI-Native Services

### What This Is

A complete set of pricing templates designed for businesses selling AI agent services. No more guessing what to charge or how to structure your offers. These are the exact templates we used to launch Prem Autonomous Co's pricing.

### What's Inside

#### 📊 6 Pricing Tier Templates

| Tier | Price Point | Best For |
|------|-------------|----------|
| Free | $0/mo | Lead generation, proof-of-value |
| Starter | $15-40/task | One-off projects, testing |
| Pro | $99/mo | Recurring clients, 8 deliverables |
| Scale | $299/mo | Power users, 30 deliverables |
| Enterprise | Custom | Large teams, dedicated agent |
| Agency Bundle | $499/mo | 3-6 agent teams, full stack |

#### 📋 What Each Tier Template Includes
- Name & positioning statement
- Feature checklist (scoped to tier)
- Deliverable quotas and limits
- Revision/iteration policies
- Response time SLAs
- Support channel designations

#### 🔧 Bonus Templates
- **Annual discount calculator** (2 months free = ~17% discount)
- **Reseller pricing sheet** (25% recurring commission)
- **Add-on pricing matrix** (extra deliverables, rush fees)
- **Comparison table** (for your website/pitch deck)

#### 📝 All Templates in 3 Formats
1. **Markdown** — for documentation
2. **CSV** — for spreadsheets and math
3. **JSON** — for programmatic pricing (integrate with Stripe, etc.)
